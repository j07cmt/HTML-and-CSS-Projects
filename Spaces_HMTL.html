Scuba Diving Certification Agencies

There are a number of agencies that provide training a certification for all levels of scuba diver, from beginner to expedition level.
Here is a list of the most popular training agencies

  • PADI
  • SSI
  • SDI|TDI
  • RAID
  • BSAC
  • IANTD

PADI (Professional Association of Diving Instructors) is the largest organisation in the diving industry, certifying more divers than all the other agencies combined. I am certified by PADI and SDI|TDI (Scuba Diving International & Technical Diving International). SDI|TDI train all levels of diverm but through TDI, they train technical divers - the ones that do it deeper, and longer.

Scuba Diving Locations

Scuba diving takes place all around the world. With the earths surface being 71% water, there are thousands of recognised dive sites around the world, in all five of our oceans. I have dived all around the British Isles, the Red Sea, and the Gulf of Thailand. I absolutely love diving with large pelagic species such as sharks, rays, and ceteceans. My main interest though is wreck diving, and my ultimate goals are to identfy a previously unnamed shipwreck by location the bell, and diving on the sister ship to the Titanic, the Britannic.
